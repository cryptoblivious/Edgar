Privacy Policy

This app is committed to maintaining the privacy and security of its users and does not collect any personal data or information. However, to better serve our users and enhance their experience, we utilize Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google. Through Google Analytics, we collect anonymous data such as user interactions, device types, and app usage patterns. This data helps us gain insights into app usage trends, identify areas for improvement, and optimize the overall user experience. Rest assured that all data collected through Google Analytics is handled in compliance with applicable privacy laws and regulations, and we do not collect personally identifiable information.

It's important to note that while our app does not collect personal data, Google Play may collect certain data as outlined in their Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review Google Play's Privacy Policy to understand how they collect, use, and protect your information. By using our app, you acknowledge and agree to the collection and processing of data by Google Analytics and Google Play for the purposes mentioned above. Your trust and privacy are our top priorities, and we continuously strive to improve our app based on user feedback and responsible data analysis.
